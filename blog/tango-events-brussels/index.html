Tango Events in Brussels & Around

Sonja & Sven April 1, 2020 6 min read

Where can I go to practice tango? What are the nicest tango events and milongas in Brussels and around? Belgium's vibrant tango scene offers dancers the opportunity to go out dancing every night of the week in most major cities.

Finding Tango Events in Belgium

Belgium has a thriving tango community with numerous milongas, practicas, and workshops happening throughout the week. Two primary event calendars will help you discover all the tango activities:

  • Milonga.be - Features local milongas, practicas, and workshops, with a concentration of events in Brussels
  • Tango.be - Provides comprehensive calendar coverage of tango events across Belgium

Featured Milongas in Brussels

La Milonguita

One of the most beloved milongas in Brussels, La Milonguita is hosted monthly or bi-monthly on Saturdays. The venue features beautiful wooden flooring that's perfect for dancing. You can find this elegant milonga at:

Rue Montoyer 1, 1000 Brussels

Tango Bar

For a more intimate weekly experience, Tango Bar offers Wednesday evening gatherings. The cozy atmosphere makes it perfect for both experienced dancers and those just starting their tango journey.

Rue de Dublin 30, 1050 Brussels

El Dorado

A charming monthly Sunday milonga located just outside Brussels, El Dorado provides a welcoming environment for tango enthusiasts from across the region.

Kerkstraat 251, 1851 Grimbergen

El Regalo

A high-level milonga that regularly features professional tango demonstrations by accomplished dancers. This venue attracts experienced dancers seeking quality music and dancing.

Ducpétiauxlaan 133a, 1060 Sint-Gillis

Pianofabriek

Located in the heart of Saint-Gilles, Pianofabriek hosts Sunday afternoon milongas in a unique cultural space that combines art, music, and dance.

Rue du Fort 35, Saint-Gilles

Maison du Peuple

Saturday milongas at Maison du Peuple feature excellent wooden floors that make for perfect dancing conditions. The historic venue adds character to your tango evening.

Parvis de Saint-Gilles 37, 1060 Saint-Gilles

Major Tango Festivals in Belgium

Brussels Tango Festival

With over 15 years of tradition, the Brussels Tango Festival is an international event that attracts dancers and maestros from around the world. The festival utilizes prestigious venues including the magnificent Concert Noble and even features performances on the iconic Grand Place.

This multi-day festival offers workshops with world-renowned teachers, milongas that run late into the night, and spectacular shows that showcase the best of Argentine tango.

Antwerp Tango Festival

Celebrating more than 10 years of bringing tango to Antwerp, this festival is hosted at the beautiful Zaal Athena. The Antwerp Tango Festival attracts dancers from throughout Belgium and neighboring countries.

Tango Marathons Near Brussels

La Cita de los Amigos

This bi-annual marathon has become a favorite among tango dancers in Belgium. The La Cita de los Amigos marathon takes place twice a year:

  • Winter Edition: Held at the atmospheric Brigittines church in Brussels
  • Summer Edition: Takes place at the historic Kurhaus in Spa, offering dancers a weekend getaway

Marathons are intensive tango events where dancers gather for an entire weekend of continuous dancing with minimal workshops, focusing purely on the joy of social dancing.

Palace Cita

A more intimate marathon experience, Palace Cita is hosted at a historic cinema in central Brussels. The unique venue provides excellent acoustics and a cozy atmosphere for marathon dancers.

Join the Brussels Tango Community

Whether you're a beginner curious about your first milonga or an experienced dancer looking for regular dancing opportunities, Brussels and the surrounding area offer something for everyone. The welcoming community, excellent venues, and frequent events make Belgium a wonderful place to pursue your passion for Argentine tango.

Start by attending a few milongas to get a feel for different venues and atmosphms. Don't be shy—the tango community is known for its warmth and willingness to help newcomers feel at home on the dance floor.

For up-to-date information on current events, check Milonga.be and Tango.be regularly. See you on the dance floor!
