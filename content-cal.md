## Image Guidelines

Every new blog post needs 2–4 optimized images:
- **Format:** WebP preferred, JPEG acceptable
- **Max size:** 150KB per image
- **Filename:** descriptive, hyphenated, lowercase — e.g. `tango-beginners-class-brussels.webp`
- **Alt text:** descriptive with location — e.g. `"Argentine tango beginners class at BE-TANGO Brussels"`
- **Location in repo:** `be-tango-rebuild/images/blog/`
