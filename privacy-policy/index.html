Privacy Policy

Overview

BE-TANGO maintains this privacy policy to inform visitors how their information is collected and protected. The policy became effective on May 23, 2018, and is subject to updates.

Data Collection

BE-TANGO gathers the following information:

  • Class, workshop, and event participation records
  • Name and contact details (including email)
  • Personal data such as postal code, birth date, age, height, language preference, and phone number
  • Customer survey and offer-related information

Cookie Usage

Purpose: Cookies facilitate website navigation and track popup displays to limit them to a maximum of three showings per visitor.

Definition: "A cookie is a simple, small file that is sent along with pages of this website and by your browser."

Google Analytics: The website uses Google's Analytics service to monitor visitor behavior. Google may share data with third parties if legally required, and information is stored on U.S. servers.

Personal Information Management

Protection: BE-TANGO pledges not to sell, distribute, or lease personal data to third parties.

Access Rights: Individuals may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their data by emailing the organization.

Cookie Deletion: Users must manually delete cookies through their browser settings.

Newsletter and Class Signup Agreements

By registering for newsletters, free trial classes, or paid tango classes, users consent to receive promotional and non-promotional email communications.
