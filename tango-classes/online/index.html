Frequently asked questions

Do I need a partner for online classes?

+

For many technique exercises (pivots, balance, posture), you can practice solo. However, for the figures and connection work, a partner is recommended. We designed many exercises specifically to help you improve even without a partner.

Do I need a lot of space?

+

Not necessarily. We designed many exercises, like the modified cuadrado, specifically for smaller spaces like a living room. While some traveling steps benefit from more space, most of our content works perfectly in a typical home environment.

How do I access the online classes?

+

Once you subscribe, you'll receive login credentials to access our online platform. You can stream videos directly or download them for offline viewing. The platform works on computers, tablets, and smartphones.

Can I combine online classes with in-person lessons?

+

Absolutely! Many of our students use online classes as a supplement to their in-person lessons. The online content allows you to review what you learned in class, practice at home, and accelerate your progress. It's the perfect combination for serious learners.

How long do I have access?

+

Access is subscription-based. As long as your subscription is active, you have unlimited access to all content. We regularly add new lessons, so you'll always have fresh material to explore.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

+

Perfect! Our "Absolute Beginners" section starts from zero. We assume no prior dance experience. Each lesson builds on the previous one, and we explain everything in detail. Start with the Basic Cuadrado and work your way up at your own pace.

Is there support if I have questions?

+

Yes! You can reach us via email with any questions about the content. For more personalized feedback, we recommend combining online lessons with occasional private sessions or attending our group classes when possible.
