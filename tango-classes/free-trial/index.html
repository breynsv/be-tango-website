Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your free trial class

Do I need to bring a partner?

+

No! The majority of our students come alone. We rotate partners during class so everyone gets to dance with different people. This is actually better for learning!

What should I wear?

+

Wear comfortable clothes that allow you to move freely. For shoes, any comfortable shoes with smooth soles will work (avoid running shoes or shoes with rubber soles). Many students wear dress shoes, flats, or dance sneakers. No special dance shoes needed for your first class!

I have two left feet. Can I still learn tango?

+

Absolutely! Everyone starts somewhere, and our beginner classes are designed for people with no dance experience. We break everything down into simple steps and progress gradually. You'll be surprised how quickly you pick it up!

Is the trial class really free?

+

Yes, it's completely free with no strings attached. We want you to experience what tango is like before committing to a course. There's no obligation to sign up after your trial class.

How long is the trial class?

+

The free trial class is one hour long, which is the same duration as our regular classes. This gives you a real feel for what our courses are like.

What age groups attend your classes?

+

Tango is for everyone! Our students range from their 20s to their 70s. The beauty of tango is that it can be enjoyed at any age and fitness level. Most of our beginner students are between 30-60 years old.

What happens after the free trial?

+

After your trial class, you can decide if you'd like to join our beginner course. We'll provide all the information about course schedules, prices, and how to register. There's no pressure - take your time to decide!

Can I take more than one trial class?

+

The free trial is offered once per person. However, if you'd like to try both locations (Brussels and Woluwe) before deciding, please contact us and we can arrange this.

What if I can't make the scheduled trial date?

+

No problem! Free trial classes are offered at the beginning of each course cycle. Contact us and we'll let you know about upcoming dates that might work better for you.

What Happens Next?

The journey from your free trial to becoming a tango dancer

1

Your Free Trial

Experience your first tango lesson. Meet the instructors, try the basic steps, and feel the music.

2

Join Our Beginner Course

If you enjoyed your trial, join our 14-week Start-to-Tango course. Learn all the fundamentals in a structured, progressive way.

3

Continue Your Journey

Progress to intermediate levels, attend practice sessions (practica), and eventually dance at milongas (tango social dances).

4

Join Our Community

Become part of Brussels' vibrant tango community. Make friends, attend events, and enjoy the magic of tango for years to come.
