Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your free trial class.

Do I need to bring a partner?

+

No! Most of our students come alone. We rotate partners during class so everyone gets to dance with different people. This is actually better for learning!

What should I wear?

+

Wear comfortable clothes that allow you to move freely. Smooth-soled shoes work best — avoid running shoes. No special dance shoes needed for your first class.

I have two left feet. Can I still learn tango?

+

Absolutely! Everyone starts somewhere. Our beginner classes are designed for people with no dance experience. You'll be surprised how quickly you pick it up!

Is the trial class really free?

+

Yes, completely free with no strings attached. We want you to experience tango before committing to a course.

How long is the trial class?

+

The free trial class is one hour long — the same as our regular classes. This gives you a real feel for what our courses are like.

What age groups attend your classes?

+

Tango is for everyone! Our students range from their 20s to their 70s. Most of our beginner students are between 30–60 years old.

What happens after the free trial?

+

After your trial, you can decide whether to join our beginner course. We'll provide all the information on schedules, prices and registration. No pressure — take your time!

What if I can't make the scheduled trial date?

+

No problem! Contact us and we'll let you know about upcoming dates that work better for you.

What Happens Next?
