WHY TANGO

Why Argentine Tango Makes the Perfect Wedding First Dance

Argentine tango is one of the most visually stunning partner dances in the world — and it is surprisingly well-suited to weddings.

Unlike most ballroom dances, tango is built on close connection, improvisation, and musicality. Even a simplified, learner-friendly choreography looks elegant and confident to wedding guests. There is no need for flashy kicks or complicated lifts — the beauty of tango lies in the embrace, the pauses, and the shared movement.

Our instructors Sonja & Sven danced tango at their own wedding and have since created wedding choreographies for many couples — from complete beginners to experienced social dancers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many lessons do we need for a wedding dance?

+

We typically recommend 5 to 10 private lessons depending on your starting level and the complexity of the choreography you'd like. Couples with no prior dance experience often feel confident with 8–10 lessons. Those with some background may need only 5–6.

Do we need any prior dance experience?

+

No prior dance experience is needed. We design the choreography to match your level and comfort. Our instructors Sonja & Sven have guided many couples — from complete beginners to experienced dancers — through a beautiful wedding first dance.

Can we choose our own song?

+

Absolutely. We build the choreography around your chosen song. We can work with traditional Argentine tango music, modern songs with a tango feel, or even a completely different genre. The dance is tailored to the music and your personality as a couple.

How far in advance should we book?

+

We recommend starting 3–6 months before your wedding date. This gives enough time to learn the choreography, practice it comfortably, and refine it without pressure. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible — contact us to check availability.

What style of dance do you teach for weddings?

+

We specialise in Argentine tango — an elegant, connected partner dance that looks stunning for a wedding first dance. We adapt the style to suit you: classic and romantic or more dynamic and dramatic. The close embrace and flowing movements of tango create a unique and memorable moment.

What is the price for a wedding dance package?
