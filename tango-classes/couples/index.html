Looking for Dance Classes in Brussels as a Couple?

Argentine tango is unlike any other dance. It is an improvised conversation between two people — no fixed choreography, just deep listening, connection, and mutual trust. Couples who dance tango together discover a whole new way of communicating without words. BE-TANGO has been teaching Argentine tango in Brussels since 2007, with 151 five-star reviews.

Why Tango is the Perfect Dance for Couples

Build Connection

In the tango embrace you literally tune in to each other — no distractions, just full focus on your partner and the movement. That quality of attention carries right off the dance floor too.

Share a Passion

Break the weekly routine with a real night out. Brussels has fantastic milongas every week where you can go dancing together, meet new people, and keep discovering new music and movements.

No Experience Needed

Two left feet? No problem. Our Start-to-Tango course is designed for people who have never set foot on a dance floor. We take it gently, step by step.

A Dance for Life

Tango never gets boring. You keep refining your technique, musicality, and connection. It is a journey of discovery you share together for years — always something new to explore.

You Do Not Need to Come as a Couple

Most of our students — including those in couples — come to their first lesson alone. We rotate partners during class, so everyone dances with multiple people. This is actually one of the fastest ways to improve. Your partner is welcome to join at any time, and many couples start at different moments and catch up quickly.

If you want to start together, that is great too. We will place you at the same level and make sure you have plenty of time to practise as a pair during class.

Questions About Couples Dance Classes

Do we both need to sign up to join tango classes as a couple?
You are welcome to enrol separately — there is no requirement to come as a pair. We rotate partners during class, so each person learns at their own pace. If you want to learn together with one-on-one focus as a couple, our private lessons are the ideal choice.
Do we need any dance experience to join as a couple?
Not at all. Our Start-to-Tango beginner course starts completely from zero — solid posture and walking together. Anyone can learn this.
Can one of us sign up without a partner?
Absolutely. Most students come alone. We rotate partners during class, so you will dance with many different people — which is actually great for faster learning. Your partner is very welcome to join later.
What makes tango a good dance for couples?
It forces you to leave the world outside for a while and truly focus on your partner — leading, following, feeling, letting go. It is a wonderful shared experience that connects you without a single word needing to be said.
Is there a free trial for couples?
Yes! Each of you can attend a free trial class with no obligation. You can both attend the same trial class together.
How much do dance classes for couples in Brussels cost?
The beginner course (Start-to-Tango) costs €195 per person for 14 lessons. Prefer to learn at your own pace as a couple? Contact us and we will arrange a private lesson.
