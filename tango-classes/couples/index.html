Looking for Dance Classes in Brussels as a Couple?

Argentine tango is unlike any other dance. It is an improvised conversation between two people — no fixed choreography, just deep listening, connection, and mutual trust. Couples who dance tango together discover a whole new way of communicating without words.

BE-TANGO has been teaching Argentine tango in Brussels since 2007 with 141 five-star reviews. Whether you come as a couple or alone, our structured courses take you from absolute beginner to confident social dancer.

Why Tango is the Perfect Dance for Couples

Build Connection

Tango teaches you to listen to your partner without words. The dance is built on trust, embrace, and subtle communication — skills that extend beyond the dance floor.

Share a Passion

A shared hobby you can both enjoy for life. Tango is a social dance — you can go out to milongas together, meet new friends, and keep discovering new music and movements.

No Experience Needed

Start completely from zero together. Our beginner course (Start-to-Tango) is designed for absolute beginners and runs in a relaxed, encouraging atmosphere.

Dance Together for Life

Unlike many other forms of exercise or entertainment, tango keeps evolving. There is always something new to discover — from musicality to new technique — making it a lifelong activity for couples.

You Do Not Need to Come as a Couple

Most of our students — including those in couples — come to their first lesson alone. We rotate partners during class, so everyone dances with multiple people. This actually makes you a better dancer faster. Your partner can join at any time, and many couples start at different points and catch up quickly.

If you want to start together, that is great too. We will place you at the same level and ensure you have plenty of time to practise together during class.

Questions About Couples Dance Classes

Do we both need to sign up to join tango classes as a couple?

+

Yes, both partners enrol together for group classes. However, you do not need to come as a couple — many students come alone and dance with rotating partners in class. If you want to learn specifically as a couple, private lessons are also available.

Do we need any dance experience to join as a couple?

+

No experience is needed at all. Our Start-to-Tango beginner course starts completely from zero and is ideal for couples discovering dance for the first time.

Can one of us sign up without a partner?

+

Absolutely. Most students come alone. We rotate partners during class, so you will dance with many different people. This is actually great for faster learning. Your partner is welcome to join later.

What makes tango a good dance for couples?

+

Tango is a conversation between two people — it builds trust, non-verbal communication, and connection. Couples who dance tango together often report improved communication and a shared activity that is both physically engaging and creatively fulfilling.

Is there a free trial for couples?

+

Yes! Each of you can attend a free trial class with no obligation. Book your free trial — you can both attend the same trial class together.
