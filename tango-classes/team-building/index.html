A Team Building Experience Unlike Any Other

Argentine tango is fundamentally about two people working together — one leads, one follows, both listen. These dynamics mirror the skills every great team needs: trust, communication, adaptability, and the courage to lead and to let others lead.

BE-TANGO offers custom tango group workshops for companies in Brussels. Whether as a one-off team event or a series of sessions, we tailor each experience to your group size and goals. No dance experience required.

What Tango Teaches Every Team

Trust & Vulnerability

Tango requires you to trust your partner — physically and mentally. This is one of the most powerful exercises for building genuine trust in a team, without any artificial "fall and catch" games.

Non-Verbal Communication

Most of what happens in tango is communicated through body language alone. Teams discover how much information they miss when only listening to words — and how powerful non-verbal clarity can be.

Leadership & Followership

In tango, both roles are equally skilled and equally important. Participants rotate between leading and following, developing empathy for both positions — a key insight for any workplace.

Shared Fun & Laughter

Beyond the insights, tango team building is simply a lot of fun. Participants leave energised, having shared something genuinely new together — which is exactly what good team events should do.

How a Session Works

1

Introduction

A short explanation of the tango principles that apply to teamwork: the lead-follow dynamic, the role of listening, and the idea of improvised dialogue.

2

Partner Exercises

Guided exercises where participants work in pairs — no prior experience required. Focus is on communication, sensitivity, and mutual adaptation.

3

Group Practice

Everyone dances together in a social format, rotating partners. The energy in the room builds as people discover they can actually dance tango — even in a single session.

4

Debrief & Reflection

Optional group debrief connecting the tango experience to your team's real dynamics. Which insights surprised people? What can be brought back to the workplace?

Questions About Tango Team Building

Do participants need any dance experience?

+

No experience is needed whatsoever. Tango team building sessions are designed for complete beginners. The focus is on the soft skills — trust, communication, and collaboration — not on learning to dance perfectly.

How large can the group be?

+

We can accommodate groups of varying sizes. Contact us with your group size and we will tailor the session accordingly. Sessions can be held at our Brussels venue or at your own company location.

What does a tango team building session involve?

+

A typical session combines a short introduction to Argentine tango principles, guided partner exercises that build trust and communication, and a fun social dancing experience. Our instructors guide the entire session — no preparation needed from participants.

Can the session be held at our company location?

+

Yes, we can come to your venue if you have a suitable space with a clear floor area. We can also host the event at our Brussels dance studio. Contact us to discuss options.

How much does a tango team building session cost?

+

Pricing depends on group size, session duration, and location. Contact us with your requirements and we will provide a personalised quote.
