What You'll Learn

Our comprehensive beginner program covers all the essentials

Foundation & Posture

Learn the correct posture and embrace (abrazo) that forms the foundation of Argentine tango. We focus on comfort, connection, and body awareness.

Basic Steps & Walks

Master the fundamental tango walk and basic step patterns. You'll learn to move with confidence, balance, and musicality.

Partner Connection

Develop the unique connection between leader and follower. Learn to communicate through embrace, creating a dialogue without words.

Musicality & Rhythm

Understand tango music structure and learn to express the music through your movement. Discover the difference between tango, vals, and milonga.

Basic Figures

Learn essential tango elements like the ocho (figure 8), giro (turn), and parada. Build your vocabulary of movements step by step.

Social Dancing Skills

Learn milonga etiquette and floor navigation. We'll prepare you to join the social tango community with confidence.

Why Learn Tango?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a dance partner?

+

No! Most of our students come alone. We rotate partners during class so everyone gets to dance with different people. This is actually beneficial for learning, as you experience different styles and connections.

Do I need any previous dance experience?

+

Absolutely not! Our beginner classes are designed for people with zero dance experience. We start from the very basics and progress step by step. If you can walk, you can learn tango!

What should I wear and bring?

+

Wear comfortable clothes that allow you to move freely. For shoes, any comfortable shoes with leather or smooth soles work for beginners. Avoid sneakers with rubber soles as they can stick to the floor. You can bring water, but we recommend not eating heavily before class.

Can I join mid-course?

+

If you have some basic tango experience, yes! Contact us first so we can assess your level and see if joining mid-course makes sense. For complete beginners, we recommend starting at the beginning of a new cycle (September or February).

What if I miss a class?

+

Life happens! You can make up missed classes at our other location (Brussels or Woluwe). Just let us know in advance. We also provide a brief recap at the start of each class.

What happens after the beginner course?

+

After completing the beginner course, you can continue to our First Year classes where you'll learn more advanced techniques and figures. We also have practice sessions (practicas) and regular milongas where you can dance socially.

Is the free trial class really free?

+

Yes, absolutely! Your first class is completely free with no obligation. It's a great way to experience our teaching style, meet the instructors, and see if tango is for you. Just book online or contact us.

What age groups attend the classes?

+

Our students range from their 20s to their 70s! Tango is for everyone. What matters is your enthusiasm to learn, not your age. The diversity of ages adds to the richness of our community.
