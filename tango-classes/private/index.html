Why Choose Private Lessons?

Sometimes a group class moves too fast, or too slow. Or maybe you want to prepare for the new dance season. With a private lesson, you get 100% attention from our experienced teachers.

What to expect:

  • We work specifically on what you need: from basic balance to complex figures.
  • Refining technique: Work on your posture, connection, and musicality.
  • Catching up: Missed classes? Catch up quickly at your own pace.
  • Wedding Dance: An original choreography for your big day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a dance partner for private lessons?

+

Not necessarily. You can come alone to work on your own technique, or as a couple to grow together.

How many lessons do I need for a wedding dance?

+

That depends on your experience and wishes. We usually recommend a package of 5 to 10 lessons to comfortably master a beautiful choreography.

When can I schedule a private lesson?

+

Private lessons are scheduled by mutual agreement, outside regular group class hours.

Where do the private lessons take place?

+

We offer private lessons at both our locations: Brussels (Rue du Marais 68) and Woluwe-Saint-Pierre (Avenue Orban 54). You can choose the location that works best for you.

What should I bring to a private lesson?

+

Wear comfortable clothes that allow you to move freely. Bring dance shoes or smooth-soled shoes (avoid rubber soles). If you're preparing a wedding dance, bring your song on your phone or a USB drive.

Can I combine private lessons with group classes?

+

Absolutely! Many students combine group classes with occasional private lessons to work on specific techniques or catch up on material. This is an excellent way to accelerate your progress.
