FOCUSED & INTENSIVE

Tango Workshops

Deep dives into technique, musicality, and style — for every level

What to expect from a tango workshop?

Our workshops are intensive, themed sessions that go deeper than a regular weekly class. Whether you want to sharpen a specific technique, explore a style like vals or milonga, or learn from a guest teacher, workshops give you the space and time to make a real leap forward.

Theme-Focused

Each workshop has one clear subject — no topic-hopping, just deep exploration

All Levels Welcome

We organise separate sessions for beginners and more experienced dancers

Weekend Sessions

Most workshops are held on Saturday or Sunday, in half-day or full-day format

Guest Teachers

We regularly invite guest teachers from Buenos Aires and the international tango scene

What to expect

A focused learning experience in a warm, welcoming atmosphere

Expert Instruction

Led by BE-TANGO's experienced teachers or specially invited guest instructors from the international tango circuit.

Rotation & Feedback

You'll dance with multiple partners during the session, getting varied feedback and a richer experience.

Musicality Focus

Every workshop includes attention to the music — understanding what you're dancing to is core to our teaching philosophy.

Extended Practice Time

More time than a regular class allows — so you can actually absorb and internalize what you've learned.

Structured Progression

Sessions are built logically, from foundation to application, so each step builds on the last.

Friendly Atmosphere

BE-TANGO workshops are known for their warm, non-competitive vibe. Come to learn and enjoy.
