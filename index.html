Why Choose BE-TANGO?

Argentinian tango is more than just a dance; it's a passionate journey that enriches your life in multiple ways.

A Vibrant Social Dance Community

+

Tango Argentino is inherently a social dance. It's a dance of connection, where you partner with others and share a moment of improvisation on the dance floor. Learning tango opens doors to a vibrant community of dancers, both locally and globally. You'll have the opportunity to attend milongas (tango dance events) in Brussels and beyond, where you can practice your skills, meet new people, and immerse yourself in the rich culture of tango. Discover the joy of social dancing and build lasting friendships through the shared language of tango.

A Dance for Physical and Mental Well-being

+

Learning tango offers numerous physical and mental benefits. The dance improves posture, balance, and coordination. The focus on improvisation and connection challenges your mind, enhancing cognitive function and reducing stress. The rhythmic movements and passionate music provide a form of exercise that's both enjoyable and effective. Experience the health benefits of tango and discover a dance that nourishes both your body and your mind.

A Dance of Improvisation and Artistic Expression

+

Unlike choreographed dances, Argentine tango emphasizes improvisation. It's a dance of spontaneous expression, where you and your partner create a unique dialogue on the dance floor. This focus on improvisation allows you to develop your creativity and express your individuality. Embrace the improvisational tango and discover the freedom of creating art in motion. As a folk dance, it has a deep cultural history, and every dancer can add their own unique style.

Beyond Tango: Discover Milonga and Vals

+

At BE-TANGO, we go beyond traditional tango, offering instruction in milonga and vals. Milonga, a lively and rhythmic dance, and vals, a graceful and flowing waltz, provide a diverse range of dance experiences. Learning these related dances enhances your understanding of tango music and expands your dance repertoire. Experience the variety of tango related dances.

Experience the Sheer Joy and Fun of Learning Tango

+

Above all, learning tango is incredibly fun! The passionate music, the close connection with your partner, and the satisfaction of mastering new steps create an exhilarating and enjoyable experience. Join our classes and discover the fun of tango. You will find that tango is a hobby that brings joy, laughter, and a sense of accomplishment.
