Why titles align: Every card image is a fixed height: 220px block. The title sits immediately below it with padding-top: 20px, so its top edge is always at 220 + 20 = 240px from the card top — regardless of text length in any other card. Variable description text fills flex: 1 space in the middle; the CTA link is pinned to the bottom via margin-top: auto. Cards in each row stretch to equal height via align-items: stretch on the grid.
