Experience level
First Year — Start here if… You have never danced tango, or you took a handful of beginner classes but feel you need to restart from scratch.
Second Year — Start here if… You have 1–2 years of regular classes and can dance basic sequences comfortably with a partner.
Second Year PLUS — Start here if… You have 2–3 years, attend milongas regularly, and want to add musicality and more complex figures to your dancing.
Intermediate — Start here if… You have 4+ years of tango, dance Vals and Milonga, and are comfortable with boleos and ganchos.
