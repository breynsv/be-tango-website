First Year
First Year
Your first steps into Argentine tango — open to all beginners
+

This class welcomes complete beginners and those who have taken a few classes but feel they need a solid foundation. You will learn the fundamental principles of the tango embrace, weight transfer, and the cross-system walk — the essential tools for real social dancing.

Experience required No prior experience necessary. Suitable for 0–1 year of tango.
Techniques covered
The embrace Weight transfer Walking in parallel system Cross system Forward & back ochos Parada The cross Listening to the music

Second Year
Second Year
Building vocabulary and refining partner connection
+

You can walk, do the cross, and navigate a milonga floor — now it's time to deepen your vocabulary and sharpen your musicality. This class introduces new basic patterns, works on axis and balance, and explores the connection between the music's phrasing and your movement.

Experience required Approximately 1–2 years of tango. Comfortable with the cross and basic ochos.
Techniques covered
Molinete (giro) Sacada Pivots Musical phrasing Partner connection Axis & balance Leading & following Floor navigation
View schedule for Second Year
3
Second Year PLUS
Second Year PLUS
Complex sequences and deeper musical interpretation
+

This class bridges the gap between social dancer and versatile milonga partner. You have a solid base and want to expand your repertoire with more complex sequences, off-axis movements, and a richer musical dialogue. Expect challenging, rewarding material in every class.

Experience required Approximately 2–3 years. Confident with molinete, sacadas, and musicality basics.
Techniques covered
Colgadas Complex giros Decorations (adornos) Off-axis movement Musical interpretation Layering sequences Double-time phrasing Improvisation tools
View schedule for Second Year PLUS
4
Intermediate
Intermediate / Advanced
Boleos, ganchos, enrosques — vals, milonga and free improvisation
+

For experienced dancers ready to take their tango to the milonga stage. This class goes deep into the technical and expressive dimensions of Argentine tango, covering high-energy techniques across three rhythms: tango, vals, and milonga. Full improvisational fluency is the goal.

Experience required 4+ years of regular tango practice. Comfortable with off-axis work and improvisation.
Techniques covered
Boleos (front & back) Ganchos Enrosques Vals timing Milonga traspié Complex sacadas Full improvisation Stagecraft & style
